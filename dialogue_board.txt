A: Good afternoon! I'm here to book a room for a business trip.（下午好！我来为一次商务旅行预订一个房间。）
B: Welcome, sir. When would you like to check in and check out?（欢迎，先生。您打算什么时候入住和退房呢？）
A: I'll check in on the 15th and check out on the 18th. Do you have any quiet rooms available?（我15号入住，18号退房。你们有安静点的房间吗？）
B: Let me check... Yes, we do have some quiet rooms on the upper floors. Would you prefer a single or a double room?（让我查一下……有的，我们高层有一些安静的房间。您想要单人间还是双人间呢？）
A: A single room will be fine. How much does it cost per night?（单人间就行。每晚多少钱呢？）
B: It's $180 per night, including breakfast.（每晚180美元，包含早餐。）
